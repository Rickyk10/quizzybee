## MCQ 1
Question: What is at the center of our solar system?
A) Earth
B) The Sun
C) Jupiter
D) The Moon
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: How many planets are in our solar system?
A) Five
B) Eight
C) Ten
D) Twelve
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: Earth is unique because it supports what?
