## Note 1
Sun, center of solar system, provides energy; system includes planets, moons, asteroids, comets.

## Note 2
Eight planets: terrestrial (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars - rocky); gas giants (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune - large, gaseous).

## Note 3
Earth: third planet, supports life due to atmosphere, liquid water, moderate temperature.

## Note 4
Moons: orbit planets, vary in size/composition; some may have subsurface oceans (e.g., Europa).

## Note 5
Asteroids: rocky, mostly between Mars and Jupiter. Comets: icy, glowing tails near Sun. Studying solar system reveals origins of celestial bodies.
