## MCQ 1
Question: What is at the center of our solar system?
A) Earth
B) The Sun
C) Jupiter
D) The Moon
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: How many planets are in our solar system?
A) Five
B) Eight
C) Ten
D) Twelve
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: Which of these is a terrestrial planet?
A) Jupiter
B) Saturn
C) Mars
D) Neptune
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4
Question: What makes Earth unique in our solar system?
A) It has the largest moon.
B) It is the closest planet to the Sun.
C) It supports life.
D) It is the largest planet.
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question: Where are asteroids mostly found?
A) Between Earth and Mars
B) Between Mars and Jupiter
C) Between Jupiter and Saturn
D) Beyond Neptune
Correct Answer: B
