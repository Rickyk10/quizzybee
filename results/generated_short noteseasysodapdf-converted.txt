## Note 1
The Solar System includes the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. The Sun is the center and provides energy for life on Earth.

## Note 2
Eight planets orbit the Sun. Four are rocky terrestrial planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars). Four are gas giants (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune).

## Note 3
Earth is the third planet from the Sun and supports life because it has a suitable atmosphere, liquid water, and moderate temperatures.

## Note 4
Moons orbit planets. Earth has one. Jupiter and Saturn have dozens. Some moons might even have oceans that could support life.

## Note 5
Asteroids are rocky objects mainly between Mars and Jupiter. Comets are icy objects with glowing tails when near the Sun.  Studying the solar system helps us learn about the universe.
