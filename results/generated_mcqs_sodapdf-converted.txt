## MCQ 1
Question: What is at the center of the solar system?
A) Earth
B) Jupiter
C) The Sun
D) The asteroid belt
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 2
Question: How many planets are in the solar system?
A) Four
B) Eight
C) Nine
D) Twelve
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: Which of the following is NOT a terrestrial planet?
A) Mars
B) Venus
C) Jupiter
D) Mercury
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 4
Question: What makes Earth unique in the solar system?
A) It has the largest moon.
B) It is the closest planet to the Sun.
C) It supports life.
D) It is made mostly of gas.
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question: Where are asteroids mostly found?
A) Orbiting Saturn
B) In the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter
C) In the Kuiper Belt
D) Orbiting Neptune
Correct Answer: B
